# Crooked Finger — Product Context

## What
AI-powered crochet and knitting pattern assistant. Translates patterns between formats, generates stitch diagrams, and helps crafters understand complex instructions.

## Target Users
Beginner to intermediate crocheters and knitters who struggle with pattern notation and need visual aids.

## Current State
- Pattern translation working (multi-model AI: Gemini + OpenRouter)
- Diagram generation (matplotlib + SVG)
- Deployed at crookedfinger.chandlerhardy.com

## Product Goals
- Make complex patterns accessible to beginners
- Visual explanations of stitches and techniques
- Pattern library with community sharing
- Mobile-friendly experience for crafting on the go

## What Would Advance This Product
- Pattern difficulty scoring and progression recommendations
- Step-by-step guided mode (one instruction at a time with visuals)
- Yarn/material calculator based on pattern
- Photo-to-pattern: snap a finished piece, get approximate pattern
- User accounts with saved patterns and progress tracking
